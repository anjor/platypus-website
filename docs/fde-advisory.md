## The Challenge

Most FDE programs fail because organizations:

**Hire wrong.** Standard engineering interviews miss FDE-critical traits: pain tolerance, ownership mindset, learning velocity, and client-facing capability.

**Structure wrong.** Unclear ownership between FDE and Product teams creates friction that kills deployments.

**Enable wrong.** Products that aren't FDE-ready force engineers to spend 70% of their time fighting internal systems instead of solving customer problems.
